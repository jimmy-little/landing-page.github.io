Timeline

The Very Incomplete History of Jimmy

  • 1993-1998

    James Madison University

    Major-hopping from music, to Architecture, to Set Design, to Stage Management.

  • 1998-2000

    The New York Years, Part 1

    Production manager for Studio Designs, one of New York's largest event design firms. Clients included Macy's, Gerorge Soros, some Kennedy's, the International Monetary Fund, the Democratic and Republican parties, the World Bank, and more.

  • 2000-2004

    The Tour Years

    As a union stagehand, toured with Broadway productions of Annie, Jekyll & Hyde, Cinderella, Cabaret, Seussical the Musical, Peter Pan, Dr. Dolittle, Movin' Out, Bombay Dreams, High School Musical 2, and more.

  • 2004-2014

    The New York Years, Part 2 - The Television Years

    Trading Spaces, Town Haul, Don't Sweat It, Dear Genevieve, Mom Caves, BUILT, Construction Cash, Closet Cases, and more. Lead carpenter, co-host, and project manager for hundreds of episodes of home-makeover TV.

  • 2014 and Beyond

    The LA Years

    Now living in LA and working at the Disney/ABC Television Group. Helping the Mouse House put entertainment in your pocket.
